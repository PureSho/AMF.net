# AMF Logo & Branding Guidelines

## Logo Files

- **Primary Logo**: `assets/amf_logo.png`

## Usage Guidelines

### Logo Placement
- Use the logo on light backgrounds for optimal visibility
- Maintain proper padding/clear space around the logo
- Do not alter, rotate, or distort the logo

### Recommended Sizes
- **Web Header**: 200x200px minimum
- **Favicon**: 32x32px, 64x64px
- **Social Media**: 1200x630px (Open Graph)

### Color Specifications
- **Primary Color**: Use with full color logo as designed
- Ensure sufficient contrast with background

### Best Practices
- Never use the logo on backgrounds that reduce visibility
- Maintain consistent sizing across your website
- Always credit the AMF brand when using the logo
